Welcome to Panpan Zhang's personal website. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the [Department of Biostatistics, Epidemiology and Informatics](https://www.dbei.med.upenn.edu/) (DBEI) at the [University of Pennsylvania](https://www.upenn.edu/) (PENN), under the supervision of Professor [Sharon X. Xie](https://www.dbei.med.upenn.edu/bio/sharon-xiangwen-xie-phd). Prior to join PENN, I was a Visiting Assistant Professor in the [Department of Statistics](https://stat.uconn.edu/) at the [University of Connecticut](https://uconn.edu/).

My biography
============

I grew up in Shanghai, China. I received M.A. in Mathematics from [Wake Forest University](https://www.wfu.edu/) in 2012, and started my doctoral training in the [Department of Statistics](https://statistics.columbian.gwu.edu/) at the [George Washington University](https://www.gwu.edu/) in the same year. I got Ph.D. in Statistics in 2016. My dissertation advisor was Professor [Hosam M. Mahmoud](https://statistics.columbian.gwu.edu/hosam-m-mahmoud).
